**For Marketing Teams**: Use the GTM strategy as a guide for campaign planning, content creation, and channel optimization.

**For Leadership**: Track progress against roadmap milestones, KPIs, and success metrics.

**For Product Teams**: Align product development with market needs and customer journey requirements.
