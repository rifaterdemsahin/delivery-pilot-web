Initial Partner Engagement

We begin with a comprehensive partner alignment session to understand your customers, expertise, and collaboration preferences.

Key Activities:

  • Partner onboarding and capability assessment
  • Customer profile sharing and analysis
  • Co-creation of engagement strategy
  • Definition of roles and responsibilities
  • Agreement on success metrics and KPIs

Customer Discovery & Assessment

Partners and Delivery Pilot experts conduct joint discovery sessions and assessments to understand customer needs and readiness.

Collaborative Activities:

  • Joint customer interviews and workshops
  • Technical environment assessment
  • Skills gap analysis (individual and team)
  • AI maturity evaluation
  • Infrastructure and security review

Audience Preparation & Segmentation

We work together to prepare and segment audiences based on their roles, experience levels, and learning objectives.

Preparation Steps:

  • Audience profiling and segmentation (prosumer vs enterprise)
  • Pre-workshop skill assessments
  • Customized learning path creation
  • Prerequisites communication and support
  • Team formation for enterprise customers

Workshop Design & Delivery

Partners collaborate on designing and delivering workshops tailored to customer needs and audience segments.

Joint Delivery:

  • Co-facilitation of workshop sessions
  • Real-time adaptation based on audience feedback
  • Hands-on practice with partner support
  • Q&A and troubleshooting assistance

Roadmap Development & Follow-up

We conclude with joint roadmap creation and ongoing support planning to ensure continued customer success.

Post-Workshop Activities:

  • Priority setting and milestone definition
  • Resource allocation planning
  • Follow-up support structure agreement
  • Continuous improvement feedback loop
