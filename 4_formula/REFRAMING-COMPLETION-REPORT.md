## Root Causes Identified

### ROOT CAUSE 1: Product-Market Fit Confusion
**Problem:** Marketing says "training" but reality delivers "agents"  
**Evidence:** Website emphasizes RAISE (skills gap), workshops, courses  
**Reality:** Actual delivery includes custom AI agents, workflow integration  
**Fix:** Position as cognitive infrastructure, not education platform

### ROOT CAUSE 2: Target Customer Mismatch  
**Problem:** Marketing to L&D/HR instead of engineering leaders  
**Evidence:** Value prop focuses on skills gaps, training needs  
**Reality:** Actual customers need expertise scalability in complex infrastructure  
**Fix:** Target Platform/DevOps/SRE leaders with complexity pain points

### ROOT CAUSE 3: Feature vs. Benefit Confusion
**Problem:** Selling "skills development" instead of outcomes  
**Evidence:** Features list agents but benefits mention training  
**Reality:** Value is "10x engineer effectiveness", not "learned AI"  
**Fix:** Lead with business outcomes (velocity, scale, efficiency)

### ROOT CAUSE 4: Wrong Success Metric
**Problem:** Measuring education outcomes instead of operational impact  
**Evidence:** Implied success is "people trained" or "skills acquired"  
**Reality:** What matters is delivery velocity and reliability  
**Fix:** Track operational KPIs (MTTR, deployments/week, headcount efficiency)
