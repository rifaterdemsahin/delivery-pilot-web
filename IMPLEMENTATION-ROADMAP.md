## Rollout Strategy

### Option A: Big Bang (Recommended for Consistency)
- Update all Phase 1 pages in a single release
- Ensures consistent messaging across homepage, README, pricing
- Risk: Larger change, but reduces confusion

### Option B: Gradual (Lower Risk)
- Deploy one page at a time
- Monitor for negative reactions or confusion
- Risk: Inconsistent messaging across pages temporarily

**Recommendation:** Option A - Big Bang for Phase 1
- Website is small enough to update comprehensively
- Inconsistent messaging is more confusing than big change
- Can revert entire change if needed

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Existing Customers/Prospects Confused
**Mitigation:**
- Email blast explaining the positioning refinement
- "We've always built agents‚Äînow our messaging matches"
- Offer 1-on-1 calls to existing prospects

### Risk 2: SEO Impact (Keyword Changes)
**Mitigation:**
- Keep old keywords in meta tags temporarily
- Add new keywords (Kubernetes, DevOps, platform engineering)
- Monitor search rankings, adjust if needed

### Risk 3: Message Too Technical/Narrow
**Mitigation:**
- Test messaging with 5-10 target customers before full launch
- Have backup version ready with broader appeal
- A/B test homepage hero if possible

### Risk 4: Internal Team Confusion
**Mitigation:**
- Internal training on new positioning
- Sales script updates
- Customer-facing materials updated (decks, one-pagers)
