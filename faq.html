RAISE: The Core Challenge

‚ùì What is RAISE and why does it matter?
RAISE stands for Rapid AI Increases Skills Expectations. It's the critical challenge facing modern enterprises where AI technology evolves so fast that professionals struggle to keep up. The gap between required skills and current capabilities widens daily, creating delivery gaps that threaten competitive advantage. Delivery Pilot exists specifically to solve this problem.
üéØ How does Delivery Pilot solve the RAISE problem?
We solve RAISE through a systematic three-part approach:
  • üìä Assessments: We identify your current AI maturity level and pinpoint specific delivery gaps through comprehensive evaluations.
  • üéì Workshops: We provide hands-on training on the Top 10 AI topics to rapidly upskill your team and close knowledge gaps.
  • üíª Git-Based Product Outputs: We create real project deliverables that demonstrate practical skills and close the execution gap between learning and doing.
üîç What are delivery gaps and how do you identify them?
Delivery gaps are the specific areas where your team's current capabilities fall short of what's needed to successfully deliver AI-powered solutions. We identify these gaps through:
  • Per product/program requirement 1-1 private assessment meetings
  • Analysis of Git repositories (for developers)
  • Review of prompts and workflows (for non-developers)
  • Comprehensive 48-question AI maturity assessment
Each assessment produces a unique maturity report with a tailored training roadmap.
üí° Why are git-based product outputs important for solving RAISE?
Git-based product outputs are crucial because they bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical execution. They provide:
  • Tangible Evidence: Real code and documentation that proves skill development
  • Portfolio Building: Concrete examples for professional growth
  • Immediate Value: Working solutions that enterprises can use right away
  • Continuous Learning: A foundation for ongoing improvement and collaboration
This approach ensures that learning translates directly into delivery capability.
